About Us

Sam Artworks Gallery located in Christchurch New Zealand. Our purpose is to be a great place for artworks collection. We offer experiences that strengthen and enrich our communities, and we value collaboration and dialogue.

Audiences are at the heart of all our activities, which extend out from the Gallery’s home to all the Christchurch. We provide visitors with fresh ways to think about artworks and participate in creativity. Expandinng your aesthetic horizons through our remarkable collection . Joining the conversations generated by our innovative visitor events, outreach activities and education programmes, and become part of our connected community.

Our collection currently number over 1000 artworks. Together, they include major holdings of New Zealand and global historic, modern and contemporary artworks.

Sam Wazowski
Director, Sam Artworks Gallery

Our Purpuse

We offer thousands of artworks collection from all over the world. we actively contribute to making beautiful artworks an easier access to people living in our communities; we support innovation, experimentation, imagination and empathy through art; we supply a good learning environment for all the art lovers and students groups in our communities.

Our Ambition

We always offer authentic and meaningful experiences for people through artworks; We'd like to share beliefs that through artworks we know ourselves, each other and the world, more deeply. And we hope to make a powerful contribution to the cultural life ofChristchurch and New Zealand by collaborating with creative communities to offer extraordinary artworks experiences that engage, challenge and inspire diverse audiences.
